5. Who has checked out more than 1 item? 
Tip: Research the GROUP BY syntax.

	Anand Beck.

	SELECT m.name
	from book b
	join checkout_item c on b.id = c.book_id
	join member m on c.member_id = m.id
	group by c.member_id
	having count(c.member_id) > 1
